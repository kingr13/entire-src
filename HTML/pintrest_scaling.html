Sharding  > CLustering

Problems with clustering
 
  • New  and  Complicated

  • Little community support  / Skilled people

  • Cluster management Algorithm is an extremely complex piece of code affecting whole of the cluster : Single point of failure!




Benefits of sharding 


  • 1. Horizontal partitioning leads to high Availability, load balancing, row splitting. 

  • 2. Relatively a simpler operation.

  • 3. Supported by  (the ultra robust) MySQL ( http://www.mysql.com/products/cluster/scalability.html )
